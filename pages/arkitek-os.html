How ArKiteK Works

1

Capture

Identify signals, boundaries, goals, and pressures in your domain. What must happen? What must not? What direction are we pushing?

2

Compile

Qualifiers turn raw signals into machine-readable rules. Strength, confidence, and scope become explicit guidance your AI can follow.

3

Execute

Deploy the compiled Context Playbook alongside your AI system. Every decision is traced back to its source and can be audited.

4

Export

Share your Context Playbook with new teams or AI systems. No lock-in. No retraining. Same rules apply everywhere.

Key Concepts

Signals

Observable patterns and events your system notices: goals, pressure, risks, constraints, exclusions.

Context Elements

Strength-graded authority objects ranging from hard constraints to soft signals of preference.

Qualifiers

Metadata that makes signals measurable: strength, confidence, scope, disposition, frequency.

Context Playbook

The portable artifact of your decision system. Export it, version it, share it. Your system, everywhere.
